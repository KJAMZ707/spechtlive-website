DJ R3$pecht

Weddings, private parties, and festival sets, played by a working touring audio technician who also happens to be a DJ. DJ R3$pecht brings his own sound, lighting, and staging gear to every event — no rented-out middleman, no guesswork on compatibility — plus the artist-friendly instincts of someone who's spent a decade on the other side of the stage. Currently holding a standing residency at the Blue Lake Wave Lounge and playing events throughout Humboldt County.
