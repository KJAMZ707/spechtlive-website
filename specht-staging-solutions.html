Specht Staging Solutions

Full-service sound, lighting, and staging for venues, planners, bands, and fellow performers across Humboldt County. Built on 10+ years of touring and festival production experience — including a run with 8th Day Sound and freelance work at Coachella and the Hollywood Bowl — Specht Staging Solutions brings festival-grade gear and technique to local stages. Whether it's a full FOH build for a live band, a lighting rig for a venue, or a one-off rental with an engineer to run it, every job is handled by someone who's built these systems at scale and knows how to make them work under pressure.
